Controls:

'w'/'s' move camera forward/back.

'a'/'d' turn camera left or right.

Up arrow/down arrow: look up or look down.

'g' turns on god mode.  God mode allows movement in all three dimensions.

'f' turns off god mode. Allows only movement along the x/y plane (the 'ground').

'Esc' exits program.
